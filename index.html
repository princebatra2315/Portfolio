What we do?

We focus on Interview Preparation for Final and pre-final year students along with developing problem solving capabilities in first and second year students and prepare them for ACM-ICPC, Google Code Jam, Facebook Hacker Cup and other competitive events.

What is the need?

The coming future is going to be dominated by automation in every aspect, and every such thing will require a code to run.

Interview Preparation

Most of the tech giants like Amazon, Samsung, Microsoft, Adobe look for the strong knowledge of Data Structure & Algorithm with Projects. So most of their interview rounds rely on problem solving using Data Structure, Internships and Algorithms.

Best To Learn Early

Learning to code is similar to learning a second language. The earlier the children are exposed to fundamental topics, the more deeply they will absorb these concepts.

Confidence Building

It is empowering for children to be able to create projects themselves and learn from them. It builds confidence and self- esteem.
